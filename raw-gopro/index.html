Raw pictures with a GoPro Camera

There is a hack to shoot pictures in .raw encoding using GoPro HERO3 Black camera.
In order the hack to work, the camera will take a picture 5 seconds after the boot and it will render a .raw that will take the camera around 40 seconds, it will stop rendering the .raw picture when it takes another photo. Then it turns off.
So the camera acts as in One button mode. The GoPro will generate three files:
  • A .JPG image
  • A .RAW image (the file is large, ~20MBs)
  • A .txt text with information about the raw image such as tone curve, gray something, blah blah blah
  • And... It also generates lots of .bin useless files
