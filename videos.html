VIDEOS

I posted videos in youtube since 2012, one year later google banned my account because of the music. 300 videos lost, 75 subscribers, 30.000 views... ALL IS LOST!

I contacted google but they told that this cannot be undone. I searched in all my machines, pendrive, google drive, dropbox, nokia lumia.

And I found 25 videos, thats 7% of the total!

As in the HOME page says, I like videoediting!

I shoot with the GoPro HERO3 Black Edition and the GoPro HD HERO2 Outdoor Edition.

I post videos related with GoPro, sport, hacking, DIY mounts and accesories, dev stuff, tutorials, etc.

I edit with Final Cut Pro 7 and some other software like Apple's COLOR or Adobe After Effects.

I also have a Vine account, where I publish 6 seconds looping videos shot with a Nokia Lumia 520 using 6Sec, a unofficial Vine client, that is better than the official Vine app, but I dont shoot only with Nokia Lumia in Vine, I upload GoPro videos to Vine using Vine client.
