GoPro HERO4 Future Camera

Some icons may reveal features of the GoPro HERO4 camera

While copying the GoPro bmp icons of my Hero3 Black from the port z:\ to the SD card, (Port z is where all the files that the gopro use such as bmp icons, sounds, binary and stuff) I found all of the HERO3 Black icons, but I also found some icons that shouldnt be there...:

Perhaps the features are a future update for HERO3/HERO3+? (dont think so, nor hero3 nor hero3+ have the chip to connect to internet) But most probably these icons are the features of the A9 ambarella chip based GoPro HERO4!
