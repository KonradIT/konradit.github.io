GoPro camera research

2013 - Present

I have been developing scripts for GoPro cameras and researching their WiFi API calls. I first got a GoPro in 2012, loved it and decided to extend the use by writing an ambarella shel script to turn the LED on when it booted, followed by extending the timelapse interval. Some of the highlights:
  • Successfully extend the timelapse interval on HERO2/HERO3 cameras, which was used by several high profile clients.
  • Made the Ambarella Shell script that enabled the HERO3 Black camera to shoot long exposures.
  • Currently maintain the unofficial GoPro API (goprowifihack), a great resource for hobbyists and companies.

GoPro scripts collection: konradit/autoexechack
Unofficial GoPro WiFi API: konradit/goprowifihack

Freelance Android Developer

2014 - Present

Android OS is my preferred OS for smartphones and smartwatches, I develop apps for Android and Android Wear, with Java and slowly migrating to Kotlin.
In 2015 I got the LG G Watch, the first Android Wear device in order to explore the capabilities of a slimmed down Android OS for wrist computers. The apps I make are mostly apps I need on my watch, apps that allow me to be more productive or that take too much time to use in a phone, also I make apps that make the Android Wear OS better. I am also experienced with Rooting/AOSP ROM hacking and Google Cardboard/GearVR.
Some of the apps I made:
  • CamControl for GoPro: A unofficial GoPro WiFi client, allows full control of the camera from the phone and Android Wear watch, also uses Material design. Link
  • Theater Time: Automate Theater mode for Android Wear devices. Link
  • WearAlert: A Tasker plugin to send alerts to the Android Wear device. Link
