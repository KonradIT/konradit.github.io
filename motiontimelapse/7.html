2 hour water hose timer timelapse device


Leave the kitchen and go to the garden.
Not only egg timers spin slowly, garden water timers spin two times slower than the egg timers. In 2 hours a water timer can spin 360 degrees.
Downside: not easy to modify, and not so much documentation.
Here is a video of brynmag, a GoPro helper.


You can find a 2 hour hose timer in any hardware store, or on ebay.

Swipe left and right using the left and right arrow keys
