Other panning devices


Here are other timers that can also make your gopro spin in an hour:


This epic setup by Flareside from the gopro user forum.
Timer on left is the driver unit. Timer on the right is a free spinning empty shell. (It was already broken so I didn't hurt it by yanking out the gears.) When the rubber band between them is in a figure 8 it rotates the camera CW and when running a simple oval around the two units, it rotates the camera CCW. The timer on the right could be replaced with anything round and free spinning. And by changing its diameter, it will it will also change the rotational speed of the panning head.

This one made by the admin Rambo, its a "egg" egg timer with a ball head on the top, a very good idea.

Swipe left and right using the left and right arrow keys
