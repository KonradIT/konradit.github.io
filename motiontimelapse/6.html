2 Panning devices stacked together - 360x2 in 1 hour


This one is mine, what I did is I used a Ikea STAM and a chicken egg timer, modified the same way as the stam.
Any panning devices that have a bolt at the top and are not the gopro mount device will do.
What two timers do is pan two times 360 degrees in one hour, 360 degrees in half hour.

Swipe left and right using the left and right arrow keys
