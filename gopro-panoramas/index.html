GoPro Panorama hack - rotated pictures

The GoPro HERO3 Camera takes pictures up to 12 MP with wide angle horizontally but not vertically.

So if you rotate the camera 90 degrees it will take portrait pictures, but not rotated.

With the hack installed, the camera will take portrait rotated pictures.

You need a pan motor, or you can rotate slowly your tripod head.

Here is a picture of my setup:

Note:

If the camera is rotated with the wifi button poining UP you need the 270 degrees picture rotation hack.
But if the wifi button is pointing DOWN then you need the 90 degrees picture rotation hack.

Download:

270 degrees picture rotation hack
90 degrees picture rotation hack

How to:

The camera will act like in one button mode with timelapse.
So, when you are ready, turn the camera on, wait for a picture to be taken, move the device you are using to rotate the camera (a motor, for example), then wait for another picture, move, wait for picture move... When you are finished turn the camera off.
